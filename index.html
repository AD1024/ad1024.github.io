Mike He


I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, Princeton University advised by Prof. Aarti Gupta. Before joining Princeton, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Washington. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Zachary Tatlock on formal methods, machine learning systems and equality saturation. My research focuses on building accessible tools for enabling practical formal methods on real-world systems.
